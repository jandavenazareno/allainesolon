ABOUT US

Workbench Construction Inc., started in 2002 as a single proprietor firm. It provided construction services to private corporations and individuals in real estate and telecommunications industries. On February 24, 2005, the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) approved its conversion into a Corporation as a Design+Build Company. Workbench Inc. has gathered a team of professional Engineers, Architects and Project Managers to cater to a wide range of services in Residential Construction industry. A support of site engineers, office engineers, CAD operators, design renderers, foremen and skilled workers adds to the professionalism of the team. Mission: Workbench Inc. exists to build houses that transform ideas into actual homes. We aim to construct houses efficiently and give clients the best value home within their budget. We also exist for our employees and their families to have a dignified life. Vision: Workbench Inc. will be the first choice in custom build homes for families living in Southern Manila, Laguna, Cavite and Batangas Area.
